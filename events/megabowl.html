MegaBowl

When is this epic showdown?

Each match time will be announced, along with the commanders that will be participating. The Tournament will be on August 1st.

Creators you say?

Of course! Several amazing Magic: The Gathering content creators will be sharing their time and creative energy to make some super cool, but not overly high-powered, EDH decks to bring Magic alive in a tournament.

Each creator team will be choosing one of the commanders they spun the Mega Wheel for, and crafting a deck around them.
